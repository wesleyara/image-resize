Custom Favicon Generator

To replace the default favicon, you can:

  1. Use an online favicon generator like favicon.io
  2. Create a 32x32 pixel PNG image with your calculator icon
  3. Replace the file at public/favicon.ico

Suggested Design:

  • Background: Purple gradient (#9333ea to #3b82f6)
  • Icon: White calculator/resize symbol
  • Style: Modern, minimal design
